# epi-use employee app user guide
Dandre Vermaak

## logging in
the system uses **corporate email** for id's. 
- **admin login:** `admin@epiuseapp.com`
- **password:** `EpiUse123!`
- *security note: admins can reset passwords from the main registry.*

## core registry (employee list)
- search anything: name, role, email, or employee #.
- view individual profiles and compensation (if you have the clearance).
- export the entire registry to CSV for external auditing (download icon, top right).

## visual hierarchy view
this is the map of how the company works.
- **"global entity" (virtual root):** you'll see a blue "epi-use" box at the very top. this is a container for all managers who report directly to the board (no manager assigned).
- **pan & zoom:** use your trackpad or mouse wheel to move the map.
- **select personnel:** click on any card to see their full profile in the sidebar.
- **drag and drop:** if you are an admin or hr, you can drag a person's card and drop it on another to reassign who they report to instantly.

## staff enrollment (onboarding)
- fill out the "add employee" form with birth dates and salary.
- select their **reporting line manager** from the list.
- if they are the ceo/orphan, leave the manager selection blank.

## credentials & security
- users can update their passwords under the "change password" tab.
- system admins have the authority to purge (delete) records or toggle a staff member's "active" status.
